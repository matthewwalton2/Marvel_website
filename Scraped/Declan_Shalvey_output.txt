In 2008 he illustrated Classical Comics' adaptation of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein, and drew two issues of the Irish comic Freakshow (Atomic Diner) in 2007 and 2009. In 2009-2010 he drew ten issues of IDW's 28 Days Later.
