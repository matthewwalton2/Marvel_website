In 1985-86 hired to do backgrounds for Neal Adams at Continuity Comics. In 1986 was hired by John Romita for an on staff position as a "Romita Raider" . This is where I cut my teeth in the business, learning everything there was to learn. I started to do freelance work, as well as backgrounds for Keith Williams.
