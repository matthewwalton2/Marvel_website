His earliest Marvel work was on Champions and Super-Villain Team-Up.,Hall is the original artist and co-creator of the West Coast Avengers team. Hall also served as the regular artist on Avengers Vol 1 and penned issues of Amazing Spider-Man Vol 1. Bob Hall also served as assistant editor to Jim Shooter at Marvel Comics. Hall later joined Shooter at Valiant Comics.
