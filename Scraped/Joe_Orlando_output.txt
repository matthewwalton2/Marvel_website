Orlando later joined Wood at E.C. comics.  When EC went out of business, Orlando drew science fiction and mystery stories for Marvel Comics in the 1950's.  At this time, he also did work for MAD Magazine.  After a period of painting and advertising owrk, Orlando returned to Marvel Comics in 1964.  He drew three early issues of the superhero series Daredevil.  Mr. Orlando also had noted acclaim for his art on pirate stories.  Alan Moore is one noted admirer.
